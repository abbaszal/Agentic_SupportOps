# Support SLA & Escalation (Internal)

## SLA Targets
- Low priority: first response within 48 hours.
- Normal priority: first response within 24 hours.
- High priority: first response within 4 hours.
- Urgent: first response within 1 hour.

## Escalation Triggers
- Safety issue or potential hazard: escalate immediately.
- Repeat issue after repair within 30 days: escalate to Tier 2.
- Customer premium tier + high priority: escalate if no resolution within 24 hours.

## Response Style
- Be concise and empathetic.
- Confirm understanding, state next steps, provide timelines.
- Cite the relevant policy section when making decisions.
