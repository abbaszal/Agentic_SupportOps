# Warranty Policy (Internal)

## Coverage
- Devices: 12 months limited warranty from purchase date.
- Batteries: covered for 12 months if failure is due to manufacturing defect.
- Accessories: 6 months.

## Not Covered
- Normal wear and tear.
- Water damage, physical impact, unauthorized repair.
- Cosmetic damage that does not affect function.

## Replacement Rules
- If covered and within warranty: offer replacement or repair.
- If repeated failure within 30 days of repair: escalate to replacement approval.
- Keep customer informed of timelines and required diagnostics.

## Required Checks
- Confirm order date and product category.
- Request troubleshooting steps completed (if relevant).
- If diagnostics suggest defect: approve replacement.
