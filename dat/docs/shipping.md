## Delays
- Weather, customs, and carrier disruptions may cause delays.
- If shipment is delayed > 7 business days after promised window:
  - Apologize and offer expedited replacement shipment if stock allows (premium tier prioritized).
  - Consider a goodwill credit for premium tier.

## Lost Packages
- If tracking shows delivered but customer reports not received:
  - Verify address and delivery photo (if available).
  - Open carrier investigation.
  - Offer replacement if investigation supports claim.

## Address Changes
- Address changes are only possible before the label is created.
