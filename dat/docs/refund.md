# Refund Policy (Internal)

## Eligibility
- Standard tier: refunds allowed within 14 days of delivery for unused items in original packaging.
- Premium tier: refunds allowed within 30 days of delivery.
- Digital services are non-refundable after activation.

## Exclusions
- Physical damage not covered by manufacturing defect.
- Missing serial number or proof of purchase.
- Items returned without RMA approval.
